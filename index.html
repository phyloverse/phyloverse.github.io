About me

Ph.D candidate, University of Wisconsin-Madison

Evolutionary botanist from the Global South interested in the systematics, biogeography, and evolutionary history of Neotropical epiphytic, hemiepiphytic, and arboreal plants. Currently, a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Botany at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, investigating the evolution of floral characters in Moraceae and the systematics/hybridization/taxonomy of Ficus sec. Americanae from phylogenetic and geometric morphometric approaches.
