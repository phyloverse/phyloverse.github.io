About me

Ph.D student, University of Wisconsin-Madison

Hi, I'm Nicole Mitidieri Rivera, a Peruvian botanist interested in the systematics, biogeography and evolutionary history of Neotropical epiphytic, hemiepiphytic and arboreal plants. Currently a doctoral student in the Department of Botany at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, investigating the evolution of floral characters in Moraceae and the systematics of Ficus sec. Americanae from phylogenetic, developmental, and geometric morphometric approaches.
